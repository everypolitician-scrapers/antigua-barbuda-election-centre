Election Candidates 2014

Candidates are chosen by local parties, through national party organisations - which must approve local candidates. Candidates are not required to live in the constituencies in which they run; the candidate with the most votes wins the constituency seat. A total of 41 candidates will compete in the 2014 General Election (Read more @ Caribbean Elections Today).
